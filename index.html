    Jumpstart to College!


    Welcome to Jumpstart to College! Calling the college application process stressful is an understatement. But, we want to make that a bit easier and more accessible for everyone. This site is packed with information, calendars, and resources for all students entering the college aplication process - first-generation, low-income, private school and public school students. We also have information for teachers to assist them in writing reccomendations and ensuring student success!
