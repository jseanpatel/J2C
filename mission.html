Our Mission

About us

This year, about 17.0 million students will attend undergraduate programs in the United States. But, only 12% of them will be first-generation, low-income students.

National Center for Education Statistics 2019-2020

J2C aims to bridge the gap that students from marginalized families face. By allowing students a resource to quickly and easily plan out their college process, even students who the first in their family to apply to college can have the knowledge to jumpstart their application process.

The J2C official website was made for Google CSSI.
The source code is licensed MIT and is available on GitHub.
